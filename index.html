Hi there!

I'm an aspiring data scientist and AI researcher seeking new opportunities at like-minded companies. I'm passionate about novel methods of generating insight from time-series and spatial data, as well as anything to do with High-Performance Computing (HPC).

Right now, I'm a team member on the Supercomputing Competition team for the San Diego Supercomputer Center as well as an independent researcher working under the Halicioglu Data Science Institute at UCSD. I also manage the projects committee of the Data Science Student Society at UCSD and work on avionics for the university's Rocket Propulsion Lab, helping to put the first collegiate liquid-fueled rocket into space.

In my free time, I enjoy reading new ML articles, coaching high school competitive debate, and (attempting to) cook new things.
